# Toast

A transient notification that appears at a corner of the screen. Auto-dismisses after a configurable duration, or can be persistent.

## Accessibility

- Rendered as `role="status"` (non-interrupting) by default; use `role="alert"` for critical errors.
- The dismiss button has `aria-label="Dismiss notification"`.
- Prefer short messages that are meaningful without additional context.
