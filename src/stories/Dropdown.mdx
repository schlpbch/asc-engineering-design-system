## Accessibility

- The trigger button has `aria-haspopup="true"` and `aria-expanded` reflecting open state.
- The menu panel has `role="menu"` and items have `role="menuitem"`.
- Keyboard: `Enter`/`Space` opens; `Escape` closes; `ArrowUp`/`ArrowDown` navigate items.
